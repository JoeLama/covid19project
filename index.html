Wash your hands properly

Washing your hands is one of the most important steps to curbing Covid-19. The illustration shows you how you can wash your hands the right way as one of the preventive measures against this deadly disease.
Use a sanitizer whenever possible. When washing your hands, ensure you wash thoroughly with soap and water

HOW TO WASH HANDS PROPERLY

Masks are critical in curbing Covid-19

Always make sure you wear a mask before stepping out in public.
Masks assist in preventing particles from other people (or in the air) from coming into direct contact with your face. This lowers the possibility of infection. Do not share masks and always ensure they are worn properly to be effective.

HOW MASKS PREVENT DISEASES

Always maintain social distance

When in public, maintain social distance (at least 6 feet) between two people.
Avoid coming into contact with people who have flu-like symptoms. Avoid hugging or kissing as this will facilitate the spread of the disease. Covid-19 virus is spread through touch and through coughing/sneezing. If you maintain a healthy social distance, you can play your role in eliminating this menacing threat.
